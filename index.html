Cooking Ex Optimizer

A kitchen companion for iOS that tracks every temperature at once and projects when each one will reach its finish target. Steak, oven, sous vide and dough share one screen, with live countdowns that adjust as new readings come in.

What it does

  • Track multiple temperatures side by side, each with its own target.
  • Live projection of when the next target will be reached.
  • Hands-on chart: drag to inspect, pinch to zoom, tap a reading to edit.
  • Camera capture reads digits off a thermometer or oven display.
  • Variables, experiments and outcomes for the original Experiment Optimizer toolkit.
  • Works fully offline. No accounts. No subscriptions.

Privacy

Cooking Ex Optimizer stores all data locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded, shared, or transmitted to any server. There are no analytics, no ad trackers, and no third-party SDKs.

The camera is used only when you open the camera-capture sheet. Frames are processed on-device by Apple's Vision OCR; only the recognized digits are written to your reading list. No image, video, or text is stored or sent off the device.
