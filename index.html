Cooking Ex Optimizer

Track every cooking temperature at once. The chart projects when each one will hit its finish target, so steak, oven, sous vide and dough all share one screen.

Drag to scroll - tap a screenshot to enlarge in your browser.

Multiple tracks side by side

Each track has its own name, target, and timeline. A summary strip shows live readings and countdowns for every track at once, so you can keep tabs on everything from a single screen.

Live projection

The app fits a curve through your readings and extrapolates. The countdown ticks down in real time as new data refines the model. A green dot moves along the curve to show where things stand right now.

Hands-on chart

Drag across the chart to inspect any time and temperature. Pinch to zoom the time axis. Tap a reading to edit it. Plus extends the forecast, minus brings the last reading back to the center.

Camera capture

Point your camera at a thermometer or oven display and the app reads the digits using the on-device Vision OCR, then logs them automatically.

Beyond cooking

Keeps the original Experiment Optimizer toolkit: define variables, get adaptive suggestions for the next experiment to run, record outcomes, and review live model confidence with clear explanations.

Works offline

All data lives on your device. No accounts, no subscriptions, no analytics. Export and import everything as JSON for collaboration.
